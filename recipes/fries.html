Classic French Fries

Description

Delicious crispy French fries, a classic favorite for all ages!

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into sticks or wedges.
  2. Rinse the cut potatoes under cold water and pat dry.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a pot to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Add potato sticks to the hot oil in batches and fry until golden brown.
  5. Remove fries and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  6. Sprinkle salt over the fries while hot.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
