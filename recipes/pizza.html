Homemade Margherita Pizza

Description

Enjoy the classic flavors of Margherita Pizza with fresh ingredients and homemade dough.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into your desired shape.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Tear fresh basil leaves and scatter them over the cheese.
  7. Drizzle a bit of olive oil over the top and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a minute, slice, and serve.
